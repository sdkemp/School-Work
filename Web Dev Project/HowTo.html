How To

Want to go shoot clay targets yourself?

It's easier than you think. Here's a quick checklist of things you need before going.

1.

Decide what type of game you want to play

You should do some research on what type of game interests you. Look online, watch some videos, and do some research to see what piques your interest. There are a ton more clay target sports than the ones covered on this website, and there is for sure one that fits for you.

2.

Find a shotgun range near you

Once you have decided what game best suits you, you next need to look for a shotgun range near you. You'd be suprised at how many ranges are actually around you. Once you have found a place that looks good to you, you should double check some things. First, check whether or not the range you have selected is open to the public. Some ranges can be members-only, and it's better to save yourself your time, and make sure beforehand. After this, you need to then check if the range has shotguns available for rent. This of course does not apply to you if you have your own, but if you're a first timer, you don't want to show up to a range, and not be able to shoot.

3.

Go Shooting!

After arriving to your chosen range, check it, rent a shotgun if you need it buy some ammo, and then you're good! Going clay target shooting is alot than you think. Don't forget the rules of gun safety, and most importantly, have fun.
