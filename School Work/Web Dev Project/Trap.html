Trap Shooting

Another one of the many sports in Clay Target Shooting, is trap shooting. It invovles a field with 5 different stations, but only one house, called the trap house.

If you would like to know more, an interactible field is available below, just click on a section to expand it.

The clay target house for trap holds only one clay target thrower, but the thrower itself is constantly rotating. Once the shooter calls pull, the house will fire a target in a random direction, adding difficulty to the shot.

A game of trap consists of 5 different stations, with each player rotating between each station. Each player will take a single shot at a station, and then rotate to the right. The round is over when the shooter has completed 25 shots.

Trap consists of 5 different stations that all are at widening angles towards the trap house. A game of trap can be played by up to 5 people at a time, with each player moving to the rightmost station after they take a shot. The game can also be increased in difficulty, if the player wishes, by standing further back in the lane. A typical station has an automatic thrower that will listen to a shooters call.
